Alimamy Lewis Seray Conteh

Mechanical Engineer & STEM Enthausiast

Alimamy Lewis Seray Conteh is a mechanical engineer from Fourah Bay College, University of Sierra Leone, with a Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering Degree (Honours). He is a co-founder of the STEAD Society, the Sierra Leone National Point of Contact for the Space Generation Advisory Council (SGAC), and the National Outreach Coordinator for Sierra Leone at the International Astronomical Union's Office of Astronomy Outreach.

Alimamy is also part of the African Astronomical Society Outreach team and a member of the Sierra Leoneans in Technology (SLinT).
